Pro Tips
⚡
Use This Tracker
Your Tracker page pings you the instant any product flips to In Stock via ntfy.sh — the fastest way to know before it sells out. Set the interval to 3 min for best coverage.
🐦
Follow @PokemonRestocks
The @PokemonRestocks account on X/Twitter and Threads posts drop links in real-time. They run automated trackers for every major retailer and alert followers as soon as stock changes.
💬
Join the Discord
TrackaLacker Discord (29k+ members) and PokéBeach community forums post real-time drop info. Community members share cart links and success tips the moment drops go live.
🛒
Pre-stage Your Cart
Keep the product in your cart at all major retailers ahead of drops. Have a saved payment method and shipping address. On drop day, just hit checkout — every second counts for PC ETBs especially.
📱
Mobile App Alerts
Enable push notifications for the Target app, Walmart app, and Best Buy app. All three send in-stock alerts for wishlisted items. Combined with your ntfy.sh tracker, this gives you four simultaneous alert channels.
🏪
Don't Sleep on In-Store
In-store stock replenishment follows vendor routes (Tue–Thu for Walmart/Target). Check your local stores mid-week, especially early morning. Many collectors find ETBs on shelves before they ever appear online.
